## Pre-Requisites

In order to use your WalletConnect Pay, you need to obtain an App ID for your project from the [WalletConnect Dashboard](https://dashboard.walletconnect.com).

### How to obtain an App ID

1. Navigate to the [WalletConnect Dashboard](https://dashboard.walletconnect.com).
2. Select the project that is associated with your wallet (as in, the projectId that is being used for your wallet's WalletConnect integration).
<img src="/images/app-id-1.png" alt="Select the project on WalletConnect Dashboard" />
3. Click on the "Get Started" button to get an App ID associated with your project.
4. The Dashboard will now show the App ID associated with your project.
5. Click on the three dots on the right of the App ID and select "Copy App ID". You will be using this for your wallet's WalletConnect Pay integration.
<img src="/images/app-id-2.png" alt="Copy App ID from WalletConnect Dashboard" />
